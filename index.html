One-Click Timetable Import

This is the easiest and most recommended way to import your schedule.

  1. Drag this link to your bookmarks bar: Scrape VTOP Timetable
  2. Log in to VTOP and navigate to your timetable page.
  3. Click the "Scrape VTOP Timetable" bookmark you just created.

You will be automatically redirected back here to review and save your schedule. It's that simple!
